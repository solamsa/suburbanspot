Our Specialization

Targeted Roach Elimination

Roaches are resilient survivors. We use advanced biology-based strategies to target specific species and eliminate entire colonies, not just the ones you see.

German Cockroaches

The most common household invader. They breed rapidly and require multi-stage baiting and growth regulators to fully eradicate.

  • Baiting Specialists

American / Oriental

Larger, often coming from outdoor sewers or damp basements. We focus on perimeter sealing and moisture control to keep them out.
